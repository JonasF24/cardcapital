# Card Capital - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview
**Card Capital** is a trading card investment platform focused on real market data, AI insights, and portfolio analytics.

## Core Brand Assets
- Full logo: `cardcapital-logo.png` (2000x2000, transparent PNG)
- Icon: `cardcapital-icon.png` (2000x2000, transparent PNG)

## Color Palette
- Primary Blue: `#0066FF`
- Gold: `#FFB700`
- Red: `#CC0000`
- Dark Blue: `#003D99`
- Background: `#0F172A`
- Card Background: `#1E293B`

## Logo Usage Rules
- Do not distort, rotate, recolor, or overlay text.
- Use full logo for headers/docs and icon for compact UI or favicon.
- Minimum recommended display: 200px (full logo), 64px (icon).

## Voice & Tone
Professional, data-driven, transparent, and approachable.
