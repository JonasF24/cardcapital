### Step 5: Interpret the Charts

**Top Chart - Price Trend** (Area Chart)
- Shows price movement over selected period
- Blue gradient = Price history
- Uptrend = Bull market 📈
- Downtrend = Bear market 📉

**Bottom Left - Trading Volume** (Bar Chart)
- Tall bars = High activity
- Short bars = Low liquidity
- Higher volume = Confidence

**Bottom Right - Graded Cards** (Line Chart)
- Rising line = More cards being graded
- Falling line = Supply tightening
- Flat line = Stable conditions

## 📊 Key Metrics Explained

### Portfolio Overview Cards

**Total Portfolio Value**
- Sum of all your holdings in USD
- Shows month-over-month change percentage
- Example: "$260K" with "+3.2% this month"

**Cards in Collection**
- Total number of cards you own
- Example: "535 cards across 4 markets"

**Market Volatility**
- 30-day price volatility index
- Lower = Stable (safer)
- Higher = Volatile (riskier)

## 🚨 Understanding Market Alerts

### Alert Types & Actions

**⚡ High Volatility Detected**
```
One Piece market showing 15.8% growth - monitor for pullback

What it means:
  → Market rising rapidly
  → Price could drop suddenly
  → Higher risk period

Action:
  → Don't chase spikes
  → Set stop losses
  → Reduce position size
```

**✅ Strong Performer**
```
NFL Football cards trending +8.3% with stable graded volume

What it means:
  → Market growing steadily
  → Volume confirming trend
  → Healthy market conditions

Action:
  → Can add to position
  → Hold existing cards
  → Monitor for entry
```

**ℹ️ Data Update**
```
Daily feeds updated 15 min ago. Next update: 4:00 PM EST

What it means:
  → Data is fresh (< 1 hour old)
  → Reliable for decisions
  → Next refresh scheduled

Action:
  → Safe to use data
  → Check again tomorrow
  → Plan trades now
```

## ❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Risk | Solution |
|---------|------|----------|
| Chasing high volatility | Buy high, sell low | Wait for stabilization |
| Ignoring low volume | Can't exit positions | Avoid low-volume cards |
| Over-concentrating | Single market crash | Keep < 50% in one market |
| Trading without alerts | Miss key data points | Check alerts first |
| Panic selling | Lock in losses | Hold through volatility |
